Patterns & Signals

Patterns are repeated chart shapes traders use to understand price behavior. They are not guarantees. They are clues. Human beings do enjoy repeating the same mistakes in prettier formations.

Double Top

A double top forms when price tests a high twice and fails to break through. It can suggest weakening bullish momentum and a possible downside move.

Head and Shoulders

This pattern often signals a possible reversal after an uptrend. It forms with a left shoulder, higher head, and right shoulder before a neckline break.

Flags and Triangles

Flags and triangles often appear during consolidation. They can act as continuation patterns if price breaks in the direction of the larger trend.

Trend Direction Matters

A strong pattern works better when it matches the broader trend. Taking every shape you think you see is how people end up explaining losses with spiritual confidence.

Confirmation

Before entering, look for indicator support, clear structure, and a reason the trade makes sense beyond “it kinda looked good.”
