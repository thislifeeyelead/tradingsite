Indicators

Indicators are tools that help you read momentum, direction, and volatility. They don’t predict the future — they help you understand what price is already doing.

MACD

click to expand

MACD compares short-term and long-term momentum to show trend shifts.

Best use: momentum shifts + trend confirmation

Pairs with: Moving Averages, RSI

Entry ideas:

  • Buy when MACD crosses up in bullish structure
  • Sell when MACD crosses down in bearish structure

Moving Averages

click to expand

Moving averages smooth price to show trend direction.

Best use: trend direction + dynamic support/resistance

Pairs with: RSI, MACD, price action

Entry ideas:

  • Buy pullbacks in uptrend above MA
  • Sell rejections in downtrend below MA

RSI

click to expand

RSI measures momentum strength and exhaustion levels.

Best use: spotting overbought/oversold conditions

Pairs with: support/resistance, MA

Entry ideas:

  • Buy near 30 at support
  • Sell near 70 at resistance

Bollinger Bands

click to expand

Measures volatility and price expansion/contraction.

Best use: breakouts and reversals

Pairs with: RSI, volume

Entry ideas:

  • Squeeze → breakout expansion
  • Rejections at bands in trend direction

Indicator Confirmation

Indicators work best when they agree with price action. Combine structure + trend + indicator confirmation for stronger setups.
