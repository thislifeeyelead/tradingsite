Trading Indicators

Moving Average (MA)

When to use

What it does: Shows trend direction by smoothing price.

When to use:

  • Trending markets only
  • Confirm direction before entry
  • Find dynamic support/resistance

Best pairings: RSI, MACD, Price action

Entry ideas:

  • Buy: pullback to MA in uptrend
  • Sell: rejection in downtrend
  • Stronger if MA is sloping clearly

Avoid: sideways markets, blind touches of MA

RSI

Momentum indicator

What it does: Measures momentum (overbought/oversold).

When to use:

  • Spot reversals
  • Confirm momentum in trends

Best pairings: MA, MACD, support/resistance

Entry ideas:

  • Buy: RSI under 30 at support
  • Sell: RSI above 70 at resistance

Avoid: using it alone

MACD

Trend + momentum

What it does: Shows momentum shifts using moving averages.

When to use:

  • Trend confirmation
  • Momentum shifts

Best pairings: MA, RSI, structure

Entry ideas:

  • Buy: MACD crosses up
  • Sell: MACD crosses down

Avoid: choppy markets

VWAP

Institutional level

What it does: Shows average price weighted by volume.

When to use: Intraday trading only

Best pairings: volume, price action

Entry ideas:

  • Buy: reclaim VWAP
  • Sell: lose VWAP

Avoid: using on high timeframes

Bollinger Bands

Volatility indicator

What it does: Measures volatility and price extremes.

When to use: breakouts or reversals

Best pairings: RSI, volume

Entry ideas:

  • Buy: bounce off lower band in uptrend
  • Sell: rejection from upper band in downtrend
  • Breakout: tight squeeze → expansion

Avoid: assuming bands always mean reversal
